The Process

Gold verified,
ownership secured

Every Real.Gold asset undergoes rigorous independent verification before ownership is established. Modern science meets centuries of mineral law.

  • 01

    On-Site Verification

    XRF analysis, RTK geolocation accurate to 2 centimeters, LiDAR drone mapping, and core sampling programs conducted at the Courbet Mine.

  • 02

    MAPS 43-99 Reporting

    Independent third-party laboratory results verified by AIPG-certified geologists, resulting in a Verified Gold Report with precise geolocation, tonnage, and grade.

  • 03

    Earth Block Model

    Property ownership is tied to specific, traceable 5×5 meter blocks. You own the land and the defined gold within it.

  • 04

    Structured Ownership

    DST & 1031-eligible real estate interests, or tokenization via Holochain. Your choice, legally protected under U.S. property law.
